Overview

Obsidian sits at the cutting edge of this taxonomy: severe, coveted, and difficult to handle without consequence. It suggests heat gone dark, beauty made dangerous, and value that comes with risk.

Precision, volatility, appetite, exclusivity, and the kind of presence that leaves marks even when handled carefully.
